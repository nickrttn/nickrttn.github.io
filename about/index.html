About Nick

Hi, I'm Nick. Right now, I'm a student at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences, where I study Communication & Multimedia Design.

I'm interested in front-end development. I like semantics, Ruby (on Rails, Middleman, Jekyll), JavaScript (Node, Grunt, Gulp, Meteor) and Sass. I lint my code, optimize my assets and automate deployments. I usually work in Sublime Text, with a couple of Bash sessions open in GNU screen.

In a few years, I hope to be a front-end developer with serious design chops on the side.
