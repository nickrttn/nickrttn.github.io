Vaults

June 23, 2015

During the User Centered Design course we did all kinds of research. Our target domestic: “Parents”. The assigment was to come up with an addition to either the Android or iPhone default Camera or Photos applications.

My concept is called 'Vaults'. It's based on old school photo-albums. In the interviews I saw people said they missed them, so I tried to recreate them in the iPhone Photos application. The Vaults are made by 1 person. They can be transferred to someone else, whenever you are done with them. They are not shared.
